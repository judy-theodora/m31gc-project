A guide to the morphology flags

-9: saturated

-2: within 2 sigma of the stellar locus (probably a star)
-1: within 1 sigma of the stellar locus (very probably a star)

0: noise feature (more concentrated than a star)

1: within 1 sigma of the galaxy (extended object) locus (very probably a galaxy)
2: within 2 sigma of the galaxy (extended object) locus (probably a galaxy)
